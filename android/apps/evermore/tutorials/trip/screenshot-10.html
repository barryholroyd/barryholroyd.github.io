Switch to List screen (use the radio button at the top of the screen) to see the Pins (search results) in a list format.

Green arrows point towards those ahead of us; yellow towards those just ahead or to the side of us; red arrows towards those behind us. Distances are also provided.

The list gets updated in real time, so you have the option of just sitting back as you drive and waiting for something interesting to scroll into view (click on Lock to lock the list to the yellow/red arrow boundary).

Click on Faron's New Orleans BBQ to bring up its Yelp page or get directions to it.
