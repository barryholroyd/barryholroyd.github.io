Go! Tutorial Benefit
Szechuan Find the best Szechuan Chinese restaurants. Evermore helps you find the best Szechuan restaurants in a very specific area or compare the number of quality Szechuan restaurants in two or more distinct areas. By placing Targets on the map you can see how far away they are from your desired location(s) and in which directions. The translucent radius around each Target helps you easily understand what the distance is -- e.g., if a given resturant is within walking distance.
Manhattan Beach Visit Manhattan Beach, CA. When visiting an unfamiliar place, Evermore makes it easy to find friends in the area and to find interesting restaurants, activities and services that are, as a group, convenient for you. By integrating various services (such as Yelp, Google Maps and your local Contacts), Evermore also makes it easy to contact anyone you need to and/or get relevant directions.
Contacts See all your Contacts. Gain knowledge about where people you know actually live and work.
Trip Find all the good restaurants within a half mile of the freeway. Evermore can help you find all the good restaurants and gas stations near the freeway and display all the results on a single map. You can use the List screen to easily see which restaurants and gas stations are ahead of you (green arrows), near you (yellow arrows), or behind you (red arrows), as well as their distances and direction from your current location. The List screen is updated in real time, so you can sit back and drive without making any decisions until you come up on a particularly interesting restaurant that has a gas station near it.
Multiple Locations Identify best bars and restaurants around multiple locations. This can be extremely useful on trips. For instance, if attending a conference at the Salt Lake City Convention Center while staying at the Residence Inn hotel a few blocks away, you might want to identify the best bars and restaurants around each location so that you can have a meal or a drink at either one, or use the information to make plans. This allows you to easily see where one location is relative to another. For instance, when taking the local Trax light rail in Salt Lake City from the airport to downtown, it is helpful to see how far away your hotel is from the Trax station.
