App Screens and Controls

App Bar

The App Bar is located at the very top of every screen.
  • At the far left is the title, Evermore.
  • In the center are three radio buttons: Main, Map and List. These three buttons allow you to switch between the three primary screen types.
    • Main Screen
    • Map Screen
    • List Screen
  • At the far right is the app's pull-down Menu.
