Android
In 2015 I decided to specialize in Android. I devoted the entire year to studying virtually all aspects of Android and the tools supporting it. During that stretch, I produced a handful of interesting Android apps. A full list, including APKs, source code and documentation for each, is available at Android Apps.

By far, the most challenging and significant of my apps is Evermore. Evermore is a visually based, robust integration of search technologies. The implementation consists of 150 Java files with 16,000 lines of handwritten code.

Evermore is available in the Google Play Store. Extensive documentation, including a FAQ, a full reference section and a number of simple tutorials, is available as Help from the app or directly from the web site. Source code and detailed documentation regarding its architecture is also available.
