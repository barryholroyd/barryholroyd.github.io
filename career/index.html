Who Am I?

I am a seasoned engineer with extensive experience both as a hands-on developer (e.g., as an Android Staff Engineer at Quora and a Principal Architect at Oracle) and as a manager (multiple roles as a Director or VP of engineering, in charge of teams ranging in size from one to 60).

I completed my studies in 2017 and accepted a position as a staff engineer on the Android development team at Quora. I was one of two engineers working on the app. It has been downloaded by over 10,000,000 users and is rated 4.5 in the Play Store.

I am now looking for my next position. I am targeting Android-related work, either as an individual contributor or as a manager/director, somewhere in the South Bay or mid-peninsula (I live in Mountain View and would like to limit my one-way commute to about a half hour).

I deeply enjoy hands-on development, with a focus on architecture and/or refactoring -- I like a good, clean system and am willing to work hard to create one. I would also be interested in a management position, as long as it afforded me enough time to stay technically current.
