I am a seasoned engineer with extensive experience both as a hands-on developer (e.g., as an Android Staff Engineer at Quora and a Principal Architect at Oracle) and as a manager (multiple roles as a Director or VP of engineering, in charge of teams ranging in size from one to 60).

I completed my studies in 2017 and accepted a position as a staff engineer on the Android development team at Quora, followed by a position at Google managing the Google Play Services team.
